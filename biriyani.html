🍽️About us🍽️

                  Dindigul Zahir Biriyani is a popular restaurant in dindigul that offers an array of biryanis, including chicken, mutton, and egg. The biryani is cooked using the traditional dum method, which involves slow cooking the rice and meat together in a sealed pot. This gives the biryani its unique flavor and aroma. The restaurant has a cozy ambiance, and the staff is friendly and efficient 🍗🍗.

         High quality and taste are of prime importance, and because of this our brand of Biriyani and other food products have attained widespread popularity all across South India. There are numerous articles published in different journals about our Biriyani and other food products. As a result of our efforts, the word “Thalappakatti” for a common man in South India today refers to High Quality Biriyani.

Dindigul Biriyani

Legasy of Dindigul Biriyani

     The story of Dindigul Biryani traces back over 200 years to the humble beginnings of the Perumal Naidu Biryani Hotel, where the first iteration of this iconic dish was born. Founded by G Perumal Naidu, this eatery started as a modest establishment serving a concoction known as milagu kuzhambu, a pepper gravy made with mutton and spices.
